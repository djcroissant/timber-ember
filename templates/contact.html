Frequently Answered Questions

Quick answers to common workshop details.

What are your current production lead times? ▼

Our lead times vary by product size and shop queue. Slabs tables and barrel saunas generally require 6–8 weeks, which covers final hand-selection of lumber, dry kiln checks, assembly, and multi-layered finishes. End-grain cutting boards are typically dispatched within 1–2 weeks, unless custom engraving is requested.

How does nationwide delivery work? ▼

For dining tables and saunas, we custom-build heavy-duty wooden crates and ship them via freight carriers with liftgate trucks. All freight shipments are fully insured and trackable. We also offer regional White Glove delivery and setup within a 200-mile radius of Bend, Oregon, which includes inside placement, leg assembly, and packing disposal.

How do I care for my solid wood table? ▼

Our tables are finished with premium hardwax oils which seal the wood grain but allow the natural textures to breathe. For daily care, wipe with a soft cloth slightly dampened with lukewarm water and a drop of pH-neutral soap. Avoid harsh chemicals, glass cleaners, or abrasive pads. We recommend reapplying wood maintenance oil once every 12–18 months to replenish the moisture seal.

Can I specify custom metal bases or wood configurations? ▼

Yes, absolutely! If our standard customizer options don't cover your exact requirements (e.g., custom epoxy ocean resin colors, specific steel leg powder-coating codes, or unique multi-level saunas), please contact us directly using the message form above or write details in the notes section during configurator checkout.
