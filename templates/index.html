Crafted with Intent, Built to Endure

At Timber & Ember, we believe furniture shouldn't be temporary. We reject the veneer-coated cardboard of mass-market manufacturers. Instead, we work exclusively with solid lumber sourced from sustainable Pacific Northwest forests.

We use age-old joinery techniques—like floating tenons and butterfly bowties—which allow the wood to expand and contract naturally with seasonal humidity, preventing splits and warping over the years.
